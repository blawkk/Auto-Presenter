# Auto Presenter

Transform your static presentations into dynamic, narrated videos with your own voice.

## Overview

Auto Presenter is an AI-powered presentation automation tool that brings your slides to life. Simply upload your PDF presentation and a voice sample, and let AI do the rest. The system analyzes each slide, generates professional narration scripts, and synthesizes speech in your voice to create a complete video presentation.

## What It Does

Auto Presenter streamlines the entire process of creating narrated presentation videos:

- **Upload** your PDF slides and a short voice recording
- **AI generates** contextual scripts for each slide automatically
- **Review and edit** scripts to match your style and preferences
- **Voice synthesis** creates natural-sounding narration in your voice
- **Export** a polished video presentation ready to share

## Key Features

### Intelligent Script Generation
Advanced vision AI analyzes your slides and generates appropriate narration scripts that describe and explain the content naturally.

### Voice Cloning
State-of-the-art text-to-speech technology clones your voice from a short sample, ensuring the final presentation sounds authentically like you.

### Interactive Editing
Review and refine scripts for each slide with an intuitive interface. Make adjustments and regenerate audio on the fly until everything is perfect.

### Automated Video Production
The system automatically synchronizes audio with slides and produces a professional-quality video presentation ready for distribution.

## Use Cases

- **Educators**: Create engaging video lectures from presentation materials
- **Business Professionals**: Transform pitch decks into compelling video presentations
- **Content Creators**: Produce tutorial videos and explainer content efficiently
- **Remote Teams**: Share presentations asynchronously with personal narration

## How It Works

1. **Upload**: Provide a PDF of your slides and a voice sample (MP3 or WAV)
2. **Process**: AI analyzes each slide and generates narration scripts
3. **Edit**: Review and customize scripts to your liking
4. **Generate**: Voice synthesis creates audio for each slide
5. **Export**: Download your completed video presentation

## Technology Stack

Auto Presenter leverages cutting-edge AI technologies:

- Google's Gemma vision model for slide analysis
- F5-TTS for high-quality voice cloning
- Microservices architecture for scalable processing
- FFmpeg for professional video production
